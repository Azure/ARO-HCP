# Human Escalation Rules

Recommend domain-owner follow-up when:

- a change spans multiple high-risk domains
- the reviewer cannot resolve compatibility questions from code and fixtures alone
- local `OWNERS` breadth is wide and the operational blast radius is high
- the best available conclusion is medium/low confidence on a high-severity risk

When escalating, cite the owner file(s) that should be consulted rather than naming random individuals from memory.
