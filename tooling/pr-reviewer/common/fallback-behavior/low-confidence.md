# Fallback Behavior for Sparse History or Low Confidence

When history is sparse or the signals conflict:

- lower confidence explicitly
- say what evidence would resolve the uncertainty
- prefer an owner escalation over a hard claim
- do not invent historical precedent that the corpus does not support

Example posture:

> This looks risky because it broadens retry behavior across many pipelines, but I do not see enough domain-specific evidence to call it wrong outright. I would ask the pipeline owners to confirm the targeted failure modes.
